Who is Oliver Stone ?

Born on September 15, 1946 in New York, United States, Oliver Stone is a director, screenwriter, documentary filmmaker, film producer and also American actor ! (yes, that's actually a lot).
Having started his career in 1971, he has since received four Academy Awards as Best Screenwriter and Best Director (we talk about it a little further below !).
He is passionate about politics and has directed many films about it.
