And you are ?

My name is Jules DUPAQUIER, I am 18 years old and I am currently in the 1st year of BUT Professions of Multimedia and Internet, and this site is realized as part of an individual project entitled “Notes pour plus tard”.

Why this website ?

Our goal was to create a mobile first website by integrating several articles (which you could discover... I hope) integrating a film, a music, a place, and a book of our choice. We also had to add a regret and hope of our time.

What is its purpose ?

This site aims to be a kind of “time capsule” in order to leave there its interpretation of the world and the subjects that are dear to us as fresh as they are today in our mind to challenge a typical person, but also our future us.

For example, I created this site in the theme of ambition and success/ success because it is currently the main words that govern my personality and my daily life.
I want that diploma !!
More seriously, this theme refers to my entry into higher education and therefore a start in working life. In addition, I found that this theme really fit me, because I develop my desire to succeed for a long time, and I hope to continue in this objective!
